Post surgery ACL Rehabilitation


After recieving ACL surgery the rehabilitation time will depend largely on how bad the injury was and if it effected other parts of the knee for exmple the meniscus. In usual cases you will be non- weight baring for at least 3 weeks. With meniscus repair you will usually be in a straight leg brace for at least 6 weeks. This is a small guide that will give first hand tips for dealing with a torn ACL/Meniscus.


Here is a list of what you may need if you receive ACL surgery.


                  Straight Leg Brace.
                  About a million Ibuprofen.
                  Enough ice to save the polar bears.
                  Elbow Grease.
                  Alot of time on your hands.

These are some requirements

                        STAY POSITIVE
                        Be aware of crutches on wet floors.

The First Three Weeks!

THE FIRST WEEK: At this point in time you are sitting on the couch on average about 22 hours of the day. Hopefully you have wonderful family members and friends giving you some needed assistance. This is time to ice as much as possible and to slowly get off the pain medication. You can start to bend it a little bit but dont co crazy and mess everything up.

THE SECOND WEEK: After the first week is done alot of the pain is also gone. This a time to not get over confident and begin some specific knee excercises. During this time you should be doing at least 2 knee workouts a day. Some exersises that you should be doing are quadsets, straight leg-lifts, and ankle excercises. View the images below.

THE THIRD WEEK:Congrats, you made it three weeks past your surgery. This is where the real fun starts, this is usually the point where you can start to bear weight. This means that with the straight leg brace you will be able to walk and put weight on both legs. This is a very freeing point in time but you will be able to feel the weakness in the leg muscles. The most important thing at this time to regain muscle in your leg and start to strengthen.You can add many exercises to your workouts such as; calf raises, hip abductions. see below.


Week Four and on!

From this point on it is really up to you to keep excercising and strengthening. You will have little to no pain when comfortable and may only feel some pain during workouts. Especially after six weeks you will be able to do any excercise that you feel comfortable with. This is a great time to beggin injury prevention. This means even more knee excercises. You can include squats, lunges and weights now! Make sure to keep your knees strong well after your surgery. This means that your excercises will go on pretty much till you die! Enjoy your knees!
